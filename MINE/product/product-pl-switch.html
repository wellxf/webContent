PROMOTIME

Hybrid Mobile App

Brief

PROMOTIME is a hybrid mobile application, it brings the best and most up-to-date eating and drinking promotions from the best establishments in Dubai to your phone.

PROMOTIME.me uses your GPS location and lets you decide how far you want to travel, helping to make the choice easier for you.

Not only will PROMOTIME.me give you results based on your GPS location but you can then filter the results By Start Time if you’re starting early or End Time if you’re staying out late.

The application itself is small and very responsive; all information is pulled from www.promotime.me website.
